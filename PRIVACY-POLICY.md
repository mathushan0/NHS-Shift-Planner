# Privacy Policy

**MyShifts**
Last updated: April 2026
Contact: miniclawlik@gmail.com

---

## 1. Overview

MyShifts ("the App") is a personal shift scheduling tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

### Data you enter
- **Shift information:** dates, start/end times, shift types, locations, and notes you choose to add
- **Preferences:** contracted hours, pay period type, notification settings, display name, dark mode preference
- **Bank holiday region:** to correctly identify public holidays

### What we do NOT collect
- Patient names, identifiers, or any clinical information
- Health records or medical data
- Precise location data
- Device contacts, camera, or microphone data (beyond notification permissions you grant)

---

## 3. How Data is Stored

### Local storage (default)
All data is stored **locally on your device** using SQLite. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

### Optional cloud sync (Supabase)
If you choose to enable cloud sync:
- Your shift data is **encrypted in transit** (TLS) and **at rest**
- Data is stored in the **EU region** (Frankfurt)
- Sync is powered by [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a GDPR-compliant infrastructure provider
- Only your shift and preference data is synced — no health information

---

## 4. Third-Party Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Infrastructure providers (Supabase for optional cloud sync) process data solely to deliver the service, under their own privacy policies and GDPR agreements.

---

## 5. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, MyShifts uses it solely to send shift reminders you configure. Notification tokens are not shared with third parties.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Export your data** at any time via Settings → Export Data (PDF or CSV)
- **Delete your data** at any time via Settings → Delete All Data — this permanently removes all shifts and preferences from your device (and from cloud sync if enabled)
- **Withdraw cloud sync consent** at any time in Settings → Sync

---

## 7. Children

MyShifts is intended for adults in employment. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be noted in the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
